Chicken Biriyani

A tastychicken biriyani recipe

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 200g chicken
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tsp Biriyani spices
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cup water

    • Instructions

    1. Cook rice until half done
    2. In a pan, cook onions and chicken with spices
    3. Mix yogurt, then layer with rice
    4. Cover and cook until fully done.
